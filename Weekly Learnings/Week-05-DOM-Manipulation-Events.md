## What to focus on

- **Selecting:** `querySelector`, `querySelectorAll`, `getElementById`, `getElementsByClassName`
- **Modifying:** `textContent`, `innerHTML` (use carefully), `setAttribute`, `classList`
- **Creating:** `document.createElement`, `append`, `appendChild`, `remove`
- **Events:** `addEventListener`, `event.target`, `preventDefault()`; event delegation for dynamic lists
