---

## What to focus on

- **Box model:** how margin, padding, border affect layout; `box-sizing: border-box`
- **Positioning:** when to use relative vs absolute; fixed vs sticky
- **Flexbox:** `display: flex`, `justify-content`, `align-items`, `flex-direction`, `flex-wrap`
- **Variables:** `--color-primary`, `--spacing-unit` in `:root`
- **Transitions:** `transition: property duration easing`
